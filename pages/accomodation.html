    ACCOMODATION IN UGANDA

    Uganda offers a range of accommodations to suit various budgets and preferences. Here's a breakdown to entice tourists:

    LUXURY

    • Safari lodges:

      Luxurious lodges like Mweya Safari Lodge, Chobe Safari Lodge, and others offer stunning views, comfortable rooms, and excellent service.

    • High-end hotels

      Cities like Kampala, Entebbe, Mukono and Jinja have luxury hotels like the Kampala Serena Hotel, Speke Resort Munyonyo, ewa kinyoozi hotel and others.

    .

    Mid-range:

    • Boutique hotels

      Unique, stylish hotels like the Gulu Independent Hotel, mukono coner house and others offer comfortable stays and local charm.

    • Safari camps

      Tent-based camps like the ones in Queen Elizabeth National Park and Murchison Falls National Park offer an authentic safari experience.

    Budget-friendly

    • Guesthouses

      Affordable options like guesthouses and lodges are available in cities and towns.

    • Hostels

      Budget-friendly hostels like the ones in Kampala and other cities offer dorms and private rooms.

    Unique experiences

    • Treehouse lodges

      Experience the thrill of staying in a treehouse, like the ones in Budongo Forest, wakigu park, mabira forest.

    • Island resorts

      Relax on islands like Ssese Islands or Lake Bunyonyi resorts.

    Key features:

    • Variety : From luxury to budget-friendly options.
    • Local culture*: Experience Ugandan hospitality and culture.
    • Nature*: Stay in national parks, forests, or near lakes and rivers.
    • Adventure*: Choose accommodations that offer activities like safaris, hiking, or water sports.

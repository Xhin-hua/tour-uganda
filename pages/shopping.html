    THE PEARL OF AFRICA

    MY FAVOURITE TOUR DESTINATION

    SHOPPING IS A HOBBY!

    SO, LETS BOUNCE AND GET SOME BRAND NEW STUFF

    Shopping in Uganda offers a mix of traditional markets and modern malls. Here are some popular shopping destinations:

    KAMPALA CITY

    • Markets
      Markets: St. Balikuddembe Market (Owino Market), Nakivubo Market
    • Malls
      Acacia Mall, Garden City Mall, Lugogo Mall

    MUKONO highly civilized topological space.

    1. markets
      Lugazi market, kiko market, kame valey market, katosi fish market, Nakifuma fruits market
    2. Malls
      Mukono mall, Nalukenge mall

    other cities

    JINJA : Nile Mall

    ENTEBBE : Entebbe mall

    You can find a variety of products, including:

    • Local handicrafts
    • Clothing and textiles
    • Fresh produce
    • Electronics
    • International brands

    Some popular shopping experiences include:

    • Bargaining at markets
    • Buying local products like crafts, baskets, and jewelry
    • Shopping for fresh produce and spices
    • Exploring modern malls with international brands

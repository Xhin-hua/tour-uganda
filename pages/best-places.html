    QUEEN ELIZABETH NATIONAL GAMEPARK

    Location : Kasese Kamwenge Rubirizi and Rukungiri districts in Western Uganda

    This Park was created in 1952 and dubbed after Queen Elizabeth II of England in 1954. It is well-known for its abundant wildlife and breath-taking scenery. Because of its many ecosystems which include lakes and savannas it is a center for the conservation of animals.

    LAKE VICTORIA

    Location: At the border of Uganda, kenya and Tanzania
    • Meet the largest fresh waterlake in the whole world
    • The source of River Nile which is the longest river in Africa
    • The natives call it "LAKE NALUBAALE" which is a name of one their gods according to the Baganda culture

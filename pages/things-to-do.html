    Things To Do In Uganda

    HIKING

    Location: Rwenzori Mountains National Park

    - Description: Hike through the mountains and explore the diverse flora and fauna

    - Duration: 4-7 days

    - Difficulty: Challenging

    - Best time: Dry season (June to September)

    GOLLIRA TREKKING

    Location: Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

    - Description: Trek through the forest to observe mountain gorillas in their natural habitat

    - Duration: 3-8 hours

    - Difficulty: Challenging

    - Best time: Dry season (June to September)

    cultural experience

    Location: Various cultural sites and villages

    - Description: Learn about Ugandan culture, traditions, and history

    - Activities: Visit cultural sites, attend traditional ceremonies, interact with locals

    - Best time: Any time of the year

    traditional dance

    Location: Various cultural centers and villages.

    - Description: Experience Uganda's vibrant traditional dances

    - Types of dances: Baganda,basoga, banyolo, Acholi, and others

    - Best time: Any time of the year

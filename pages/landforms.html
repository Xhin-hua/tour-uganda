    UGANDA

    THE PEARL OF AFRICA

    MY FAVOURITE TOUR DESTINATION

    Uganda's landforms are diverse, with notable features including:

    • Mountains: The Rwenzori Mountains, including Mount Stanley (Margherita Peak, 5,109m), and the Virunga Mountains, home to endangered mountain gorillas.
    • Volcanic regions: The Virunga Mountains have volcanic origins.
    • Rift Valley: Part of the East African Rift System runs through Uganda.
    • Lakes: Lake Victoria, Lake Albert, and Lake Edward.
    • Plains: Savannah grasslands and plains.

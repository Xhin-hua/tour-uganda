    CITIES OF UGANDA

    KAMPALA CITY

    Capital city of Uganda

    Known for its vibrant culture, historic landmarks, and bustling markets

    • Kasubi Tombs (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
    • Uganda Museum
    • Namboole Stadium
    • Owino Market
    • ....

    Best time to visit: June to August or December to February

    JINJA CITY

    A city in Busoga kingdom

    Located in eastern Uganda Known for its adventure activities and scenic views

    • Source of the Nile River
    • White-water rafting and kayaking
    • Bungee jumping and zip lining
    • Lake Victoria cruises
    • Nyege nyege festival

    Best time to visit: Year-round, but best during dry seasons

    ENTEBBE CITY

    city of Uganda

    Location*: Central Region of Uganda, on the shores of Lake Victoria

    • - Entebbe Botanical Gardens : a beautiful garden with a variety of plant species
    • - Uganda Wildlife Education Centre: a zoo and educational center with a variety of wildlife
    • - Entebbe Beaches : a popular spot for relaxation and water activities
      • -

    Best time to visit: June to August or December to February

    MUKONO TOWN

    Capital city of Kyagwe province

    It is located about 24.5 kilometers east of Kampala, the capital city. It's a bustling hub with various educational institutions, including Uganda Christian University, Cavendish University, seeta upcoming... and a rich history dating back to the pre-colonial era.

    Mukono a population of approximately 67,290 people. having Tropical rainforest climate with warm temperatures and significant rainfall throughout the year

      key attractions

    • Ssezibwa Falls : a beautiful waterfall with cultural significance, perfect for picnics and nature walks
    • - Mukono Hills : ideal for memorable hiking and offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape
    • - Lake Victoria: the largest freshwater lake in Africa, offering opportunities for boat rides and fishing
    • - LWAJALI river source: a place to experience traditional Baganda culture, including dance, music, and crafts

    Best time to visit: June to August or December to February when am nearby!

    MBARARA CITY

    Located in southwestern Uganda

    Known for its agricultural production and scenic views and its a lazy village

    • Lake Mburo National Park
    • Mbarara University of science and technology
    • Ankole cultural experiences
    • Local markets and crafts especially cows, milk, and muzigo(cheese)

    Best time to visit: June to August or December to February

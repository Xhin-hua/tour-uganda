    UGANDA

    THE PEARL OF AFRICA

    MY FAVOURITE TOUR DESTINATION

    Explore Our Tourist Attractions

    KABAKA OF BUGANDA'S BIRTHDAY RUN

    Every 14 April, in the kingdom of Buganda, people all over the Uganda celebrate the king of buganfa's birthday in a Marathon run, every year there exists a theme attached to this run and all the money collected in that event is donated to that. for example cancer, HIV Aids....

    UGANDA MARTYR'S DAY.

    This is the largest event attend by many Ugandans and Non Ugandan tourist of different religion and faith.

    BAGISHU CIRCUMCISION

    Every male year/ even numbered year, the people of Bugishu kingdom, start their ceremony in the Bugishu palace, this ceremony is to celebrate young boys to the youth-hood stage of their life, it os composed of many interesting cultural dances.

    Enkuuka

    Every 31st DECEMBER, all the people in the Buganda kingdom converge in the Kabaka's palace to celebrate the NEW YEARS DAY in the palace of Buganda. This event brings many musician, comedians, D.J's and many entertainers. come and join the people of Uganda on ENKUUKA

    NYEGENYEGE

    This event is hoisted in Busoga kingdom, its all about entertainment, but time outside in the woods, people make camps there, dance with their celebrities for weeks. So, ensure to attend this pure African celemony

    KAMPALA CITY FESTIVAL

    This is the biggest Event in Kampala city. This event puts many stages all over the capital and most of the roads are blocked by people dancing. come a d join this pure Ugandan festival

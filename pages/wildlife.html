    UGANDA

    THE PEARL OF AFRICA

    MY FAVOURITE TOUR DESTINATION

    • Uganda has 10 national gameparks situated/located in various parts of the country . There are many different species of wild animals that live in them including birds, mammals, reptiles, amphibia and insects. So far, Murcshion falls national park is the largest where as Semiliki national park is the smallest
    • Entry into any of these game parks requires you to pay a certain fee on the entrance or directly to Uganda Wildlife Authority

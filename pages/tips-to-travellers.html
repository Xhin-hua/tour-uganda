    THE PEARL OF AFRICA

    MY FAVOURITE TOUR DESTINATION

    TIPS TO TRAVELLERS

    • Visa requirements_: Obtain a visa before arrival or upon arrival at Entebbe International Airport. Check with the Ugandan embassy or consulate for requirements.
    • Vaccinations_: Consult your doctor about recommended vaccinations, such as yellow fever, hepatitis A, and malaria prophylaxis.
    • Malaria precautions_: Take anti-malarial medication and use insect repellents, nets, and protective clothing.
    • Climate_: Uganda's climate varies; pack accordingly (lightweight clothing for warm weather, warm layers for mountainous areas).
    • Respect local customs_: Dress modestly, especially when visiting cultural sites or rural areas.
    • Safety_: Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas; keep valuables secure.
    • Health and hygiene_: Drink bottled or filtered water, wash hands frequently, and avoid undercooked food.
    • Permits and permissions_: Obtain necessary permits for activities like gorilla trekking, chimpanzee tracking, or visiting national parks.
    • Responsible tourism_: Support local communities, respect wildlife, and follow park rules.
    • Currency_: The Ugandan shilling (UGX) is the local currency; US dollars are widely accepted.
    • Language_: English is the official language, but local languages like Luganda and Swahili are also spoken.
    • Transportation_: Hire reputable taxi services or use ride-hailing apps; be prepared for rough roads.
    • Accommodation_: Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.
    • Power adapter_: Uganda uses Type G power sockets (3-pin).
    • Internet and communication_: Stay connected with local SIM cards or portable Wi-Fi hotspots.

    ADDITIONAL TIPS

    • - Be prepared for variable internet connectivity in rural areas.
    • - Respect local traditions and dress codes.
    • - Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat for outdoor activities.
    • - Stay hydrated and bring water bottles.
    • - Be patient and flexible when traveling in Uganda.

    These tips should help ensure a safe and enjoyable trip to Uganda!
